Support for Space Puzzle Academy

If you're having any issues with our app, we're here to help! Below are a few common questions and solutions. If your problem isn't listed or you need further help, don't hesitate to contact us.

1. The app won't start, what should I do?

Try restarting your device. If the problem persists, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app. Make sure you're running the latest version of iOS.
